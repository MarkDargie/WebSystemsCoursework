Payment Method Results

{{ paypiechart }}

When using the EasyBank service dashboard, users are provided with the option to tranfer payments with two methods, Secure Transfer and Express Transfer. Secure tranfers require various fields to be provided in order to make the request, such as usernames, emails and account secure codes. Secure transfers will also not be processed until the recipient has confirmed the transaction. Conversly, Express payments only require the recipients username and the amount, and will be automatically processed.
